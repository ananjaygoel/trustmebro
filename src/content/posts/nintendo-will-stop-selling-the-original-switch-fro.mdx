## What's Happening

Real talk: Nintendo has quietly locked in that it will no longer sell the original Switch, Switch Lite, and Switch - OLED Model to retailers from mid-February 2027.

It has also just dropped that sales of all consoles will cease on the official Nintendo Store at the same time. This news comes via an FAQ segment in the company's latest article regarding battery-replaced revisions for its products in Europe. (it feels like chaos)
