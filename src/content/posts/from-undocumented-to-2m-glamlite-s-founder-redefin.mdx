This incredible achievement comes from a journey rooted in significant challenges. Hernandez grew up poor and undocumented, with motherhood later inspiring her to build this multimillion-dollar empire. ## Why This Matters Gisselle's story isn't just about impressive sales figures; it's a powerful narrative of resilience and breaking societal barriers. She's actively redefining what success looks like, especially for those who start with significant disadvantages. It showcases how personal drive, even from the most humble beginnings, can lead to extraordinary entrepreneurial triumphs.
