But where a wearable can fr make a difference is during my recovery.

## The Details

Specifically, Samsung Galaxy Watch8 effectively tracks overall recovery, nutrition, and metabolic health—all of which only takes me around five minutes a day to read. First: Energy Score² can let you know how you are starting your day—rested or a bit behind the recovery eight-ball.

Energy Score with Galaxy AI uses insights based on the previous day’s heart rate, activity, and sleep to provide a snapshot of your overall health. If the score is low, Samsung Health app will provide tips to help improve it.

## Why This Matters

This number helps me determine whether the day’s activity will be based on stretching and mobility or an intense workout. Either way, I rely on my nutrition to recover. And here Galaxy Watch8 helps me easily log my food to make sure I am getting the necessary nutrients.
