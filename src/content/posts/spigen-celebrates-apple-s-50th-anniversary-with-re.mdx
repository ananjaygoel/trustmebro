## What's Happening

So basically Spigen has built a strong reputation for making some high-quality Apple accessories that also scratch that nostalgic itch.

Their C1 Collection, which resembles the famous transparent iMac G3 from the early 2000s, is one of my favorite accessories from any brand ever. (yes, really)

They then followed that up with the Classic LS iPhone case, which pulls inspiration from iconic Apple products like the original Macintosh 128K and the Apple Lisa.
