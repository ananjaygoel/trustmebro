## What's Happening

So basically Google should be ready to tell us more about a whole range of smart glasses to compete with Meta.

Heres what we know already. Last December, I wore Google Glasses in several forms while they were still under development. (shocking, we know)

Soon you ll be able to get your hands on the final versions.

## The Details

When, exactly, and for how much? We may find out more in just a handful of days.

While Meta has been the biggest tech company aiming for a place on your face in glasses form , it s far from the only one. Google s about to enter the race with a whole range of smart glasses , the company s first return to everyday face tech since Google Glass in 2013.

## Why This Matters

This time, the focus is almost entirely on AI. Gemini will be the reason and the biggest function for what makes Google s Android XR glasses work, but they ll come in a wide range of designs: Warby Parker, Gentle Monster, Kering Eyewear and Samsung are all expected to have their own models. Xreal, a maker of display glasses , will have an additional plug-in mixed reality device called Project Aura, too.

This is part of the broader shift happening across the tech industry right now.

## Key Takeaways

- This year s Google I/O developer conference is just around the corner on May 19, and we should hear a lot more about Google s smart glasses strategy.
- I ll be there, along with a number of other CNET colleagues.
- But we already know a lot, since Google talked about and demoed these glasses last year.

## The Bottom Line

The first product of this collaboration, Samsung Galaxy XR , arrived last fall. Galaxy XR is much a VR headset , but also a mixed reality computer, similar to the Apple Vision Pro and the Meta Quest 3 .
