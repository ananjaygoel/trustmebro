## The Details

Framework's modular laptops lead the PC industry when it comes to championing people's right to repair. From the original Framework Laptop 13 in 2021 to the just just dropped Laptop 13 Pro, the company has come a long way in a short while.

Providing people with configurable 12-inch, 13-inch, and 16-inch models, Framework has kept modularity, repairability, and sustainability at the core of its products. Today, you can buy a Framework laptop with a Core Ultra Series 3 or Ryzen AI 300 chip with up to 64GB of RAM and 8TB of storage, and even a model with RTX 5070 graphics.

## Why This Matters

Despite everything, but, the industry hasn't given Framework much attention. The company still commands only a thin sliver of global market , repairable laptops haven't blown up in any significant way, and the average consumer doesn't gravitate toward a Framework laptop. Buying decisions still seem to value price, simplicity, and compact designs over modular upgrades and long-term value.

Tech companies have been making moves like this as competition heats up.
