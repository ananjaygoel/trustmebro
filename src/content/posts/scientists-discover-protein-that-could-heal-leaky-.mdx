## What's Happening

Alright so Chronic stress can damage the gut’s protective lining, triggering inflammation that may worsen depression.

New research shows that stress lowers levels of a protein called Reelin, which plays a key role in both gut repair and brain health. (plot twist fr)

Remarkably, a single injection restored Reelin levels and produced antidepressant-like effects in preclinical models.
