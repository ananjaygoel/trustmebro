Stephen King is nothing if not prolific.

Even though many of the author’s books are massive tomes, with Stephen King’s longest book clocking in at over 1,100 pages, this has not stopped him from penning many of them. Since King’s debut novel Carrie was published in 1974, King has written over 66 novels and novellas, as well as five more non-fiction titles.

## Why This Matters

That is a daunting back catalog for anyone new to the writer’s oeuvre, which means many readers have asked for a primer on the writer’s work as a starting place for the King-curious. During a Reddit AMA from 2025, King himself offered a list of the three books he would recommend for someone starting a Stephen King binge, and his choices were pretty impossible to guess. According to King, the three novels he would recommend for a newcomer are Misery , The Long Walk , and Salem’s Lot .
