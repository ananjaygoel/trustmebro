## What's Happening Destiny 2: Renegades just landed, bringing a brilliant blend of the classic Bungie franchise with fresh Star Wars inspirations. This new DLC introduces "Credits," a completely new currency for Guardians to chase. You'll be earning these Credits throughout the campaign and beyond, as they're crucial for your journey. They are necessary for specific upgrades and serve as the primary way to buy items in the DLC's brand new location. ## Why This Matters This new currency changes how you'll approach progression and gear acquisition in Renegades. Credits directly tie into your ability to power up your Guardian and customize your loadout. They aren't just for show; Credits are the gatekeeper to essential upgrades and all the cool new items available. Mastering their acquisition will be key to fully experiencing the DLC's offerings. - Unlocking critical character and weapon upgrades.
- Purchasing exclusive gear and cosmetics from the new hub.
- Driving player engagement with the new location and its activities. ## The Bottom Line Credits are more than just another number on your screen; they're the lifeblood of your progress in Destiny 2: Renegades. Understanding how to earn and spend them will define your success in this Star Wars-inspired adventure. What will you spend your first haul of Credits on?
