As part of NASA’s Science Activation Program , NASA eClips engages educators and learners with standards-aligned resources grounded in authentic NASA science.

## The Details

Complementing this work, the GLOBE Program empowers participants to contribute to citizen science through environmental data collection and analysis. The partnership between these two programs creates a powerful bridge between content knowledge and real-world app – bringing Earth Systems science to life for both educators and learners.

Educators gathered for a three-hour professional learning experience on March 7 or at the National Institute of Aerospace in Hampton, Virginia. Through hands-on investigations, participants explored how land cover influences surface temperature, how clouds impact atmospheric conditions, and how soil plays a critical role in environmental systems.
