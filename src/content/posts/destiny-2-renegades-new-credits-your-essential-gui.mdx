## What's Happening Destiny 2: Renegades is here, blending the familiar Bungie universe with exciting Star Wars inspirations. This new expansion introduces a fresh currency to the game called Credits. These Credits are more than just pocket change; they're essential for your journey. You'll start accumulating them right from the campaign and continue earning them beyond. ## Why This Matters This new currency isn't just an optional extra; it's deeply integrated into the expansion's core. Credits are absolutely necessary for certain critical upgrades, meaning you can't ignore them if you want to power up effectively. Furthermore, Credits are the primary method for purchasing items within Renegades' brand-new location. This ties your progression directly to exploring and engaging with the new content. - Crucial for character progression and vital upgrades.
- Unlocks new gear and customization options from the DLC's vendor.
- Deepens player engagement with the new, Star Wars-inspired location.  ## The Bottom Line Ultimately, Credits are poised to become a central pillar of your Destiny 2: Renegades experience.
