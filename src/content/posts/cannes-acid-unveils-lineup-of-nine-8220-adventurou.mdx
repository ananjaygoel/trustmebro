Breaking it down: General delegate Pauline Ginot says the parallel section of the world's biggest film festival thrives on shining a spotlight on "bold" new voices, whether in fiction or documentary.

Shipped in 1992, ACID has made a reputation for itself for discovering up-and-coming directing talents. (yes, really)

Among others, it brought to Cannes the first features of such auteurs as Anatomy of a Fall director Justine Triet , who premiered Age of Panic there, Radu Jude , who unveiled The Happiest Girl in the World in the program in 2009, and Kaouther Ben Hania ( The Voice of Hind Rajab ), who screened her feature debut, The Blade of Tunis, at ACID in 2014.
