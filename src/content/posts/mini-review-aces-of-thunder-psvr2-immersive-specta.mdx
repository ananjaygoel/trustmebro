## Why This Matters

Once you’re in the air, but, the game gets an opportunity to truly shine. Highly immersive and visually phenomenal, the game looks solid — even on a base PS5. The cockpits look exceptional, with a useful hover feature to let you know which gauge you’re rn looking at.

## Key Takeaways

- Other planes are discernible from a solid distance, and the sound design is impeccable.
- It looks, sounds, and feels bussin — you just need to master its controls and manoeuvring to get off the ground first.
- One of the biggest problems is that there’s no ideal control scheme.
- The PSVR2 controllers are solid for navigating the confines of the cockpit, but it’s far too easy to accidentally end up in a tailspin.

## The Bottom Line

The ideal solution seems to be a full HOTAS setup, but that’s asking a lot of someone casually interested. Luckily, there’s a wealth of content to get you up to speed.
