## What's Happening

Listen up: Bandai Namco just dropped its Gashapon capsule machines will feature Pokemon keychains that honor the three starters from every mainline game starting with Pokemon Red, Blue, and Green.

These will show up in Japan starting the week of March 8, 2026, and it will be 300円 (~$2) per try. The North American site hasn’t locked in if or when we’ll see it worldwide, but Bandai Namco does tend to release these types of items everywhere. (let that sink in)
