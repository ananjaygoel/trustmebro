## The Details

I’m kicking myself for that negligence right now. Its new remake, Assassin's Creed Black Flag Resynced , reaffirms that the open-world pirate adventure is one of Ubisoft’s finest games.

Everything from its thrilling naval battles to its island treasure hunting holds up remarkably well in a respectful touch-up that mostly sticks to impactful gameplay tweaks. There is one significant change, though.

## Why This Matters

Resynced does away with the original game’s present-day storyline, a sci-fi framing that revolved around the perpetually nefarious Abstergo Industries. I was none the wiser about that at first considering that this was my first experience with Black Flag , though I couldn’t shake the nagging feeling that the story was missing a layer during my playthrough. Now that I’ve gone back and watched the missing sequences, I’m baffled by Ubisoft’s decision to leave them on the cutting room floor.

The gaming community has been watching developments like this closely.
