## What's Happening

Not gonna lie, Fanvue CEO Joel Morris built one of the creator economy's fastest-growing platforms — after giving up a teenage YouTube fortune and sweating through a Spanish villa with days of runway left.

Here's what the middle looked like. Most people see the $200 million ARR. (let that sink in)

They don’t see the summer in Spain with no air conditioning, a dwindling bank account, and investor rejection emails piling up.

## The Details

They don’t see the moment you realize you might have to tell your co-founders it’s over. That’s the part worth talking about.
