## The Details

Many organizations have already adopted AI to improve everyday productivity over the past few years. AI has rapidly become part of enterprise workflows: Building internal chatbots to answer HR, IT, or policy questions.

Using ChatGPT or Microsoft Copilot to search and summarize documents. Assisting developers with code generation and debugging.

## Why This Matters

Drafting reports, emails, meeting notes, and business presentations. These applications have undoubtedly improved individual productivity. But if we believe thats where AIs potential ends, were missing the important parts.

## Key Takeaways

- The reality is that many companies stop here and fail to tap into AIs most transformative capabilities.

## The Bottom Line

An AI agent is an autonomous system that perceives its environment, makes decisions, and takes concrete actions to achieve a goal. The key difference between an AI agent and a chatbot is that an AI agent can take actions instead of simply generating responses.
