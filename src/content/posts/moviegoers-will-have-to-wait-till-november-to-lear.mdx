Per Deadline , How To Rob A Bank , the Leitch-directed actioner starring Nicholas Hoult and Zoë Kravitz, has been bumped from its original September release date to November 13.

## The Details

The film, which features a crew of socials influencers dominating the bank-robbery space via viral videos of their crimes, was initially scheduled to open on September 4. According to Deadline , the move isnt because the film is tanking in test screenings and Leitch wants more time to tinker with it.

Its because the film is reportedly testing so well with the 18-34 demo that the studio wants the movie around for the Thanksgiving holiday. Though the movie was set to open around Labor Day, that three-day weekend, unlike Memorial Day, sees some of the lowest theater attendance of the year.
