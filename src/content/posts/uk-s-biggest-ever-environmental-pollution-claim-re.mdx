## The Details

The River Wye is one of the UKs longest and most celebrated rivers. But in recent years those living near that it regularly turns green in the summer and has become smelly and slimy.

The groups legal claim blames the spreading of chicken manure on farmers fields, and also sewage spills. It demands that action be taken to improve the state of the rivers, and compensation be paid to those whose lives and businesses have been affected.

The River Wye is one the UKs longest rivers and its catchment area is home to a large number of industrial chicken farms.
