## Why This Matters

The Nintendo London pop-up store was open in Westfield Shopping Centre for around a month last October. The store featured merchandise before only available at Nintendo‘s permanent stores in Tokyo, Osaka, Kyoto, San Francisco, and New York, including keyrings, pins, apparel, home essentials, and more. The Argos store doesn’t appear to have any of those Nintendo Tokyo exclusives for sale, but it does reportedly have items that aren’t rn available elsewhere in the UK.
