## The Details

The new agreement includes, among other things, the UEFA Nations League including the final tournament, the 2028 European Championship qualifying matches, and various friendly matches. A large portion of the games will be shown live on DAZN.

But, the matches of the respective home teams – such as the German national team in Germany – will only be broadcast with a delay as "Re-live". Alice Mascia, CEO of DAZN in the DACH region, says: “We are pleased to extend our partnership with UEFA and to offer the fandom of all nations the most comprehensive and best live football coverage.
