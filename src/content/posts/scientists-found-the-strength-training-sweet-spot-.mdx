## What's Happening

Breaking it down: Just 90–120 minutes of strength training a week may deliver some of the biggest long-term health rewards, according to a study tracking more than 147,000 people for 30 years.

That amount was linked to lower risks of death overall, particularly from cardiovascular and neurological diseases. (and honestly, same)

Combining strength workouts with aerobic exercise produced even stronger benefits.
