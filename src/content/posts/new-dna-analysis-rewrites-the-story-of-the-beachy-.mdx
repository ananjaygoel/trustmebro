## What's Happening

So get this: A Roman-era skeleton found out in southern England has finally given up her secrets after more than a decade of debate.

Known as the Beachy Head Woman, she was once thought to have roots in sub-Saharan Africa or the Mediterranean—an idea that sparked global attention. (and honestly, same)

But new, high-quality DNA analysis paints a different picture: she was most likely a local woman from Roman Britain.
