## What's Happening

So get this: Kwikset today just dropped the Aura Reach, a smart lock that offers Matter over Thread and Bluetooth connectivity.

With Matter, the lock is able to connect to HomeKit, allowing it to be controlled through Apple Home or with Siri voice commands. The Aura Reach supports hands-free auto unlock, proximity keypad wakeup, and a guided installation process for easy setup. (shocking, we know)

It is able to be activated alongside other HomeKit and Matter devices using automations in the Apple Home app.

## Why This Matters

Unlike some of the higher-end smart locks that have been coming out at CES, the Aura Reach does not include UWB, nor does it work with Apple's Home Key feature. The Aura Reach is one of several Matter-enabled smart locks in Kwikset's lineup, including the Halo Select and Halo Select Plus. Compared to those locks, it lacks Wi-Fi, Home Key, and door sensing technology that's able to determine whether a door is open or closed.
