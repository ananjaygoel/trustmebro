## What's Happening Bandai Namco has officially revealed that Ace Combat 8: Wings of Theve will launch in 2026. This highly anticipated announcement came during last night's The Game Awards, ending a significant seven-year wait for fans. The next installment in the beloved aerial combat series is confirmed for PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, and PC. This multi-platform release ensures a broad audience can prepare for new high-flying adventures. ## Why This Matters The return of Ace Combat is a massive event for its dedicated fanbase, who have been eagerly awaiting a new main entry since 2019. It signifies a fresh chapter for a franchise celebrated for its thrilling dogfights and immersive storytelling. Releasing across all major current-gen platforms means the game will be accessible to a wide array of players. This move ensures both console and PC gamers can experience the latest aerial action, expanding the series' reach. - Ends a seven-year drought for fans of the iconic aerial combat series.
- Guarantees multi-platform accessibility for PS5, Xbox, and PC players.
- Reignites anticipation for the franchise's unique blend of arcade action and dramatic narratives.  ## The Bottom Line Ace Combat 8: Wings of Theve is set to be a landmark title for Bandai Namco in 2026, promising to continue a legacy of thrilling gameplay and deep lore.
