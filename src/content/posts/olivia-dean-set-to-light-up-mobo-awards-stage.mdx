## The Details

Hip-hop star Eve is set to make her hosting debut at the show, alongside comedian Eddie Kadi, who returns for the third time. The ceremony will mark the 30th anniversary of the organisation, which celebrates black music in the UK and beyond.

It is already set to be an exciting night for London-born singer Olivia whose soulful album, The Art Of Loving, is up for four awards, including album of the year. She will be up against British trio Flo for the coveted prize, who have been nominated for their debut album, Access All Areas.
