## What's Happening

Breaking it down: Adobe says the assistant can work across apps like Firefly, Photoshop, Premiere, Lightroom, Express, Illustrator and its other apps to do tasks for you.

Last October, Adobe previewed a new assistant under the Project Moonlight moniker that could do tasks for you Adobe apps like Acrobat, Photoshop and Express. That product is now being shipped as Firefly AI Assistant. (wild, right?)

The Firefly AI Assistant will be available in public beta in the coming weeks.

## Why This Matters

The company didnt specify if the AI assistant will be priced differently from Fireflys credit-based subscription tiers.
