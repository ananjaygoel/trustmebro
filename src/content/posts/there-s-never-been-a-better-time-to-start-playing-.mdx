## The Details

The Lorcana Collector’s Guides act as a way for new players to catch up on all the lore from each of the first eight sets, broken down into two blocks of four. There is even a checklist for each set so you can physically track the individual cards in your collection, rather than doing so in the mobile app.

At a time when the TCG market is on the verge of oversaturation, Ravensburger is keeping things simple. It continues to create a welcoming environment for all players, and these collectors’ guides are the latest in a long line of smart decisions.
