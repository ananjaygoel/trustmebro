## What's Happening

So get this: In March, Apple informed the EU that it had agreed to acquire certain assets and hire employees from SigScalr, according to a notice published today on the European Commission's site.

SigScalr created the open-source observability platform SigLens, which companies can use to aggregate and analyze logs, metrics, and traces at massive scales for monitoring and debugging purposes. (yes, really)

SigLens was known for being a cost-effective and fast solution compared to many competing platforms.

Tech companies have been making moves like this as competition heats up.
