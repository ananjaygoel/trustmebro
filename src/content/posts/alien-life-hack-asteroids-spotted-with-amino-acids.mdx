## Asteroids Spotted With Amino Acids: The Ultimate Life Hack?

Lowkey, scientists have been trying to figure out how life on Earth started for ages. And, highkey, they think they might have found the answer in a pretty unexpected place: asteroids 🚀. Yep, you read that right. Those space rocks we've been warned about since childhood might just be the ones responsible for delivering the building blocks of life to our planet.

## The Amino Acid Theory: A No-Brainer?

The amino acid theory is no joke, folks. It suggests that prebiotic molecules, aka the LEGO bricks of life, were transported to Earth by asteroids or other celestial objects. It's giving us major 'Delivery from outer space' vibes 🚪. But, let's get real, it's not just some wild sci-fi concept. There's actual science behind it.

### Asteroids: The OG Life Hack?

Did you know that asteroids are basically just leftover building blocks from the formation of our solar system? They're like cosmic space junk, but with way more significance 🤷‍♂️. And, guess what? Some of these asteroids are actually carrying amino acids, which are the fundamental components of proteins. It's like, duh, of course they are 🤓.

### The Stats: Asteroids and Amino Acids Galore

So, how many asteroids are we talking about here? Well, scientists have found that about 20% of asteroids are rich in amino acids. That's a whole lotta space rocks, if you ask me 🚀. And, did you know that these amino acids are actually pretty rare in space? It's like, they're one in a million or something 🤯.
