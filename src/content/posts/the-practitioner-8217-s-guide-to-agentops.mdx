## Why This Matters

AgentOps is the set of practices, tools, and frameworks used to design, deploy, monitor, optimize, and govern autonomous AI agents in production. It extends DevOps, MLOps, and LLMOps into a domain where the software component can reason, act, and adapt independently, which means the operational challenges are qualitatively different, not just more of the same.
