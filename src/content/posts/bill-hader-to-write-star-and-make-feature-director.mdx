## The Details

(Credit: Jeff Kravitz/FilmMagic) Bill Hader is set to write, direct and star in MRC’s upcoming horror film, They Know, TheWrap has learned. Set to begin production this spring in Los Angeles, They Know centers on a divorced dad (Hader) who grows suspicious that his ex-wife is secretly dating a mysterious man who is having a strange influence on their children.

MRC is the studio financing the project. They Know is produced and Hader, via their Hanarply banner.

## Why This Matters

Alyssa Donovan will co-produce. The film is based on an original story created Duffy Boudreau (Barry). Nexus Point News first reported the news.
