## The Details

"Born Again" resumed after a time skip wherein Matt had found a new law partner: Kirsten McDuffie (Nikki M. James), a former New York assistant district attorney turned defense lawyer.

Kirsten worked alongside Matt when their firm defended Hector Ayala/White Tiger (Kamar de los Reyes) for manslaughter, a story adapted from the "Daredevil" comic arc "Trial of the Century. " But to say Kirsten stood in Matt's shadow would be an understatement.

## Why This Matters

She was so sidelined throughout "Born Again" season 1 you kicked off to wonder why she was even in the show. If she was meant to replace Foggy as Matt's partner, she fell short, but it's difficult to even call Kirsten a "rough" character because she got so little screentime to be judged. "Daredevil: Born Again" season 1 overwent a so-called "creative overhaul" that recut several episodes of the first season, excising some before-shot footage for new scenes.
