## Why This Matters

Meet CSPNet (Cross Stage Partial Network). You’ll be surprised that it can effectively reduce computational complexity while maintaining high accuracy — no tradeoff! In this article we are going to talk about the CSPNet architecture, including how it works and how to implement it from scratch.
