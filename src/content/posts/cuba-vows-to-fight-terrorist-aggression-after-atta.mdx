The incident has the potential to crank up tensions between Washington and Havana, which have been heightened since US forces seized Cuba’s key ally, the Venezuelan leader Nicolás Maduro, and the Trump administration imposed an oil blockade on the island in January. Cuba says it killed heavily armed exiles who attacked from US-registered speedboat But talks between the two countries are understood to be continuing, and both governments appeared keen to calm the situation.
