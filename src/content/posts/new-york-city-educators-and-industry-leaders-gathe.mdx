## The Details

Through hands-on sessions like aiEDU’s “Vibe Coding” and Google’s “Meet LEA,” educators explored how tools like Google AI mode and NotebookLM can spark curiosity and build AI literacy. A major theme emerged: AI’s true impact is the problem-solving it enables.

Industry leaders emphasized that as technology streamlines workflows, "human skills" — like adaptability, collaboration, and critical judgment — become essential. While embracing these advancements, attendees agreed we must remain uncompromising on privacy and equitable access.

## Why This Matters
