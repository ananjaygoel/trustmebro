## What's Happening

Here's the thing: Microsoft just just dropped something rather interesting – a voluntary employee buyout.

In short, if your age and tenure at the company add up to 70 or greater, you’re able to voluntarily leave the company, retire early, and receive a (likely) hefty payout. (yes, really)

It’s a less hostile form of layoffs, and while I don’t think Apple would implement it for the same reasons as Microsoft, I think it could serve a unique purpose for transforming the company.
