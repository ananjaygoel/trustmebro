## Why This Matters

Some instances of double health bars are more successful than others, but they serve important purposes: They reframe the boss fight experience, add new layers of challenge, and force players to (in many cases) try and try again, armed with fresh knowledge and skill. Health bars (or meters) have been used in games for more than 40 years, building on the “hit points” system introduced in Dungeons & Dragons .
