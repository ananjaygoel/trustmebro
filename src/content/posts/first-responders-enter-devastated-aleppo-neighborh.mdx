First responders entered to tend to residents, clear rubble and disarm mines. The clashes broke out Tuesday in the predominantly Kurdish neighborhoods of Sheikh Maqsoud, Achrafieh and Bani Zaid after the government and the Syrian Democratic Forces, the main Kurdish-led force in the country, flopped to make progress on how to merge the SDF into the national army.
