## What's Happening

Not gonna lie, Mbappe’s penalty eliminated Paraguay from the World Cup after a bruising game.

FIFA World Cup 2026 Kylian Mbappe’s penalty led France past Paraguay in a bruising World Cup clash played in record heat. Paraguay frustrated France in an ugly contest, played under an Extreme Heat Warning and set to go down as one of the hottest matches in tournament history. (let that sink in)

But their brave — and sometimes bruising — resistance was wrapped up when Mbappe converted from the spot, a goal that moved the France captain level with Lionel Messi in the Golden Boot race on seven goals.
