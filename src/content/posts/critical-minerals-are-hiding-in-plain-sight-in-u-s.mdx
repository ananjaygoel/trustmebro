## What's Happening

So basically Metal mines already contain large amounts of critical minerals that are mostly going unused.

Recovering even a small fraction of these byproducts could sharply reduce dependence on imports for materials essential to clean energy and advanced technology. (yes, really)

In many cases, the value of these recovered minerals could exceed the value of the mines’ primary products.

## Why This Matters

Scientists and researchers are watching this development closely.

This could have implications for future research in this area.
