Others still are arguing about whether the movie's fan service goes too far or not far enough. For me, I'm usually more worried about whether or not a "Star Wars" film uplifts me and makes me happy, but even then, perhaps we've all been missing the point.

How badly we've all been missing the point didn't become so nakedly apparent to me as it did when I was leaving my first screening of "The Mandalorian and Grogu" with my 10-year-old, and we were having a conversation afterward.
