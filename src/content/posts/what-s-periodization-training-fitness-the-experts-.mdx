## The Details

Put simply, periodization training is an intentional exercise program with “strategically fluctuating variables like volume, intensity, speed, and weight,” says strength and conditioning specialist Alena Luciani, CSCS, founder of Training2xl. It’s most commonly used coaches to help strength and endurance athletes achieve a timing-related goal, like a bodybuilding, CrossFit , or HYROX competition .

Training blocks into three distinct phases, “it allows your workouts to continuously build on one another so that you make progress faster than if your workouts are random,” Luciani adds.
