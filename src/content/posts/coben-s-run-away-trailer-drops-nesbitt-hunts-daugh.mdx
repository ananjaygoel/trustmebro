## What's Happening

Harlan Coben's prolific Netflix machine, fresh off the success of “Fool Me Once,” is back with another high-stakes thriller.

The trailer has just dropped for “Run Away,” promising a gripping new adaptation from one of the world's most successful writers.  “Run Away” stars acclaimed actor James Nesbitt in the lead role as Simon.

His world is shattered when his eldest daughter, Paige, suddenly runs away, causing everything to fall apart around him.

## Why This Matters

Coben’s partnership with Netflix has consistently delivered popular, binge-worthy mysteries that resonate with a global audience.

This new series continues that successful trend, aiming to captivate viewers with its intense narrative and intricate plot twists.  “Run Away” delves into the profound emotional impact of a parent's worst nightmare: a missing child.

James Nesbitt’s casting as Simon, the desperate father, adds significant weight and a raw, relatable intensity to the unfolding drama.  This adaptation reinforces Netflix's commitment to delivering compelling original thrillers.

Following the massive viewership of “Fool Me Once,” expectations are high for “Run Away” to be another major hit for the streaming platform.

## The Bottom Line

With a proven formula and a compelling lead, “Run Away” is poised to be another must-watch for fans of Harlan Coben's intricate thrillers.

Nesbitt's portrayal of a father on the edge will undoubtedly draw viewers into Simon's desperate quest.  Will Simon's relentless search for Paige uncover more than just a disappearance, revealing darker secrets beneath the surface?
