YouTube Premium is also a bit pricey as streaming services go, at $14/month.

## The Details

Fortunately, the company also offers a "Premium Lite" plan that's much cheaper and thanks to the recent addition of a few key features, it no longer pales in comparison to a full subscription. In fact, the $8/month YouTube Premium Lite may now be the best paid YouTube tier for most people.

YouTube Premium Lite is getting two of the more expensive tier's best features Starting today, YouTube Premium Lite now offers both background play and download options on most videos, features that were before exclusive to a full YouTube Premium plan. The update is rn rolling out, so it might take a little bit for it to reach you, but the change essentially gives you parity with a full YouTube Premium plan on the vast majority of YouTube content.

## Why This Matters

According to YouTube, the company has "heard feedback about wanting these additional features included in the service," which prompted the change.
