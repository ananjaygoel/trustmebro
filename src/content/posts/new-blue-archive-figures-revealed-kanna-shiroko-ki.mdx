## What's Happening Good Smile Company recently took to social media to unveil a fresh batch of Blue Archive figures, exciting fans of the popular game. Four new pieces of merchandise are on the way, with a strong focus on the popular Nendoroid line. Three of the four new figures will be Nendoroids, specifically featuring Kanna Ogata, Shiroko Terror, and Kisaki Ryuuge. Kisaki Ryuuge is getting extra love, appearing not only as a Nendoroid but also as a more detailed scale figure. ## Why This Matters This announcement is a big deal for Blue Archive collectors and fans, as it expands the range of available merchandise for beloved characters. It shows Good Smile Company's continued commitment to the franchise, building on previous releases. While no release windows have been shared yet, these new figures will join an existing lineup that includes popular characters such as Asuna Ichinose, Mari Iochi, and Plana. The consistent flow of new figures suggests strong fan demand and a healthy future for Blue Archive collectibles. - **More Collectibles:** Fans gain new opportunities to own figures of their favorite characters, expanding their collections.
- **Character Variety:** The selection includes both popular and perhaps some lesser-represented characters, catering to diverse fan preferences.
- **Franchise Growth:** Continued merchandise releases often signal a thriving franchise with ongoing support and potential for future content. ## The Bottom Line Good Smile Company is clearly leaning into the Blue Archive hype with these four new figures, especially with the Nendoroid focus. It's a win for collectors, but with no release dates, how long will fans have to wait to get their hands on Kanna, Shiroko, and Kisaki?
