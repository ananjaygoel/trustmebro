## The Details

"With so many insane games on Steam, players are wishlisting more and more titles," Valve explain in a blog post announcing the new feature. "Existing options like ranking and filtering are solid, but players have asked for even more ways to manage their Steam wishlists.

Today we're adding categorization as well as improved search and sharing to the wishlist. " The categories themselves are up to you.

## Why This Matters

You could do it by genre, what year they dropped, how they're reviewed, alphabetically, whether or not they're legally playable in the UK because of current age restrictions and the only way you can verify your age is through a credit card. Endless options to choose from! And then once you've made your categories and added some games to them, you can filter your wishlist to only look at dropped categories.
