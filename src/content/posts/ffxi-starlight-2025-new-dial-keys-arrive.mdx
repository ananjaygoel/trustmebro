## What's Happening Mark your calendars, adventurers: Final Fantasy XI's cherished annual Christmas event, the Starlight Celebration, is making its festive return on December 16, 2025. Square Enix has confirmed the dates, giving players a full two weeks to enjoy the holiday cheer until December 31, 2025. This year's celebration isn't just a re-run of past festivities; it introduces a brand-new mechanic designed to shake things up. For the very first time, players will encounter the Gobbie Mystery Box Festival Dial, alongside its elusive #FES Dial Keys, adding a fresh layer of excitement to the holiday season. Getting your hands on these new #FES Dial Keys is straightforward for veterans: you can earn one key daily. However, there's a prerequisite – you must have completed the Starlight Celebration event at least once in a previous year to qualify for these daily key drops. As for what treasures the Gobbie Mystery Box holds, Square Enix is keeping those specific rewards under wraps for now. ## Why This Matters The introduction of the Gobbie Mystery Box Festival Dial represents a significant move for a game with FFXI's long history. Instead of a single completion, players now have a consistent, daily incentive to participate, fostering a sense of routine and anticipation for what each new key might unlock.
