## The Details

It understands and generates both audio and speech. It also keeps the text intelligence of its backbone.

The checkpoints, along with a smaller Audex-2B, are dropped under a noncommercial license. Most multimodal models pay a text tax.

## Why This Matters

When labs add audio or vision output, text benchmarks often drop. NVIDIA research team reports this even for speech-only output models. Audex is designed to avoid that regression.

The AI space continues to evolve at a wild pace, with developments like this becoming more common.

## Key Takeaways

- TL;DR Audex is a single 30B-A3B MoE model that handles audio in and out.
- Audio inputs enter the text embedding space; audio outputs are treated like text tokens.
- Text scores match the backbone, with small gains and small losses per benchmark.
- Multi-stage SFT plus text-only Cascade RL avoids the usual multimodal text regression.

## The Bottom Line

Because the design stays simple, Audex runs on standard LLM stacks. These include Megatron-LM for training and vLLM for inference.
