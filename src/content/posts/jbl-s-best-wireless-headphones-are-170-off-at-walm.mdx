## Why This Matters

Features include everything from a volume limiter to keep your hearing safe to Smart Talk that pauses sound when you speak, and a Sound Level optimizer that evens out voices on calls. Speaking of calling, the Tour One M3s fire microphones and software combine for impressively clear calls that reduce noises around you as well as any pair in their class.
