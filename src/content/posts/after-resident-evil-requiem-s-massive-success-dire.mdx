## The Details

With a masterful control over the horror-comedy balance, surely this up-and-coming filmmaker will finally crack the code, right? Well, some the fandom still aren't sure, especially after the (supposed) leak of a divisive script .

Stinkers and big commercial flops are still a thing, and it appears movie studios are gradually figuring out staying faithful to the source material (or at least the tone and universe) are key to striking gold.

## Why This Matters

The director was quick to point out the fandom will "crucify" him if his attempt (which arrives after 2022's mid Resident Evil: Welcome to Raccoon City ) also disappoints. Trying to calm everyone down, he admits that, as a gamer himself, he's also constantly in fear of other adaptations going sideways.
