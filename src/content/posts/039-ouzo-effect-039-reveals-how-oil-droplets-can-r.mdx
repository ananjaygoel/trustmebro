## What's Happening Breaking it down: Whether its Greek ouzo, French pastis or Turkish raki, when these spirits are diluted with water, the mixture becomes cloudy. The reason for this is that the aniseed oils contained in the spirit dissolve well in alcohol but not in water. (yes, really) ## Why This Matters The clear ouzo from the bottle has a high alcohol content at which the oil is fully soluble.
