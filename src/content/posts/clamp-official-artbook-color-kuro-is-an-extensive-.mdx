Two art books appeared as a means of remembering and commemorating the pieces on display, and Yen Press picked up CLAMP Official Artbook: Color Kuro and Color Shiro so everyone worldwide could eventually see them. With Color Kuro available now, it’s clearly a gorgeous representation of the group’s work in a lavish compilation.
