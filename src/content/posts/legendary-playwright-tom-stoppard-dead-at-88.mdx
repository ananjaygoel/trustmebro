# Tom Stoppard: The OG Playwright Who Ate the Game 🍴

Lowkey, we're still reeling from the news that Tom Stoppard, the Czech-born British playwright, has passed away at the ripe age of 88.  No cap, this guy was a legend in the literary world, and his passing is giving us all the feels.

#

## A Slaying Career in Playwriting

Tom Stoppard was no stranger to the spotlight.  His plays were like, totally iconic, you know?  Arcadia, Rosencrantz and Guildenstern Are Dead, and The Coast of Utopia were just a few of his most notable works.

And let's not forget about his screenplays for Brazil and Shakespeare in Love – the guy was a master of storytelling.

## Numbers Don't Lie: Stoppard's Legacy
