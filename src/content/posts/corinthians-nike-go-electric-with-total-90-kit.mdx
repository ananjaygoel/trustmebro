## What's Happening

Corinthians and Nike are ready to electrify the football world with their brand-new Total 90 third kit.  This bold design, featuring striking black and orange detailing, is officially slated for the 2025/26 season.

It promises to be a head-turner on and off the pitch.  The launch marks a significant moment for fans of classic football aesthetics.  The iconic Total 90 line, revered for its distinctive and often aggressive styling, makes a vibrant return, blending heritage with a modern, eye-catching aesthetic.

Expect this kit to stand out from the crowd.  Adding to the excitement, the kit is set for an electric debut against fierce rivals Flamengo.  This high-stakes clash will be the first public unveiling of the “electric” design, guaranteeing maximum exposure and an unforgettable introduction to the football world.
