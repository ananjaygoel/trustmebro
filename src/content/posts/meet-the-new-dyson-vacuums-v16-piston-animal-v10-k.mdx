Dyson now has three new cordless vacuums you can shop, plus one with a Submarine head variant: the Dyson V16 Piston Animal ($980) and Dyson V16 Piston Animal Submarine ($1,100) , the Dyson V10 Konical ($500) , and the Dyson V8 Cyclone ($400) .
