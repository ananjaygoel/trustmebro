## What's Happening

Okay so Discord is taking its age verification rollout back to the drawing board.

Since it was just dropped a little over a week ago , Discord's global age verification rollout has been a bit of a mess. First, the chatroom and voice chat company had to clarify that it clutched't require all adults to upload an ID or facial scan to prove how old they are, as the initial post left many users with that impression. ## The Details

Third, users found out that Discord had partnered with Peter Thiel-backed company Persona for an experiment in the UK, which raised flags about surveillance and security, and saw some personal identifying information being uploaded to the cloud when users thought it would be processed on-device. Now, Discord is admitting that it messed up, and is trying to make amends.
