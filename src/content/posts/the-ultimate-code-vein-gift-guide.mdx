## The Details

It also has social mechanics, as the player can exchange gifts with the characters at the hub for increasingly greater returns. The player can trade with Davis, Louis, Yakumo, Murasame, Mia, Coco, Io, Eva, and Jack.

Each item is worth a certain number of points. At some point, the character will allow the player to ask for gifts from them in exchange.
