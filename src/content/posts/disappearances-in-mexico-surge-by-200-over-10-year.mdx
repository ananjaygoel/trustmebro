## Why This Matters

Montenegro’s co-worker was dropped a few hundred meters down the street, but Montenegro was driven away. As soon as she heard that her son had been taken, Montenegro’s mother, Patricia García, raced to Cuautla along with Montenegro’s wife, brother and some neighbors. Arriving at the bus stop, all they found were Montenegro’s cap and one of his tennis shoes.
