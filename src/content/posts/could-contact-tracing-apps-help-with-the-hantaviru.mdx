## Why This Matters

Contact-tracing apps were a global effort starting in 2020 during the Covid-19 pandemic. Enabled like Apple and Google , contact tracing was designed to use Bluetooth connections to detect when people had come in contact with someone who had or would later test positive for Covid and report as much. It didn’t do much to solve the spread of the pandemic, but tracking the virus became more effective at least.

This could have major implications for how we use technology going forward.
