## What's Happening

Here's the thing: The unconscious brain appears to be far more capable than scientists once believed.

Researchers found that patients under general anesthesia could still process language at a sophisticated level, distinguishing nouns, verbs, and adjectives while listening to stories. (shocking, we know)

Even more remarkably, neural activity showed signs of predicting upcoming words before they were heard.
