## The Details

The X post looks, at first glance, to just be celebrating the second anniversary of the game with some artwork depicting a bustling tavern. But, there's a character standing at the back of the fray that draws attention because of a blue glow surrounding them, and they're the only character whose face we can't see.

Their hairstyle and outfit has also never been seen before, so they're brand-new assets, and there's a letter visible on the table that translates to "sightings of griffins flying in from the northern region of Organ have been locked in.
