## The Details

When asked how he approaches new game projects, Yoko Taro answers, “It’s not glamorous, but it starts with the money (development budget). ” Yoko goes on to clarify his answer and his pragmatic approach to development.

“I’m aware from the start of how much money I have to work with, so how many resources that gets me for development time and staff, it pretty much automatically dictates what I’ll be able to make,” he explains. “The staff are definitely the most important element, because if you tell a group of veteran action developers ‘please make me something that’s not an action game,’ it’s not going to turn out well.

## Why This Matters
