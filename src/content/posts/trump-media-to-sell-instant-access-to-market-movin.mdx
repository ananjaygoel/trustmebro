## What's Happening

Listen up: Trump Media is launching a fast, paid feed to its most influential posts for Wall Street traders.

Trump Media to sell instant access to market-moving social posts Image source, Image caption, By Francisco Velasquez Business reporter Published 16 July 2026 Updated 5 hours ago Trump Media & Technology Group, which owns Truth Social, is launching a paid service to give Wall Street firms high-speed access to its most influential posts. From 1 August, instant updates will be delivered from "the highest-ranking" accounts, it dropped. (wild, right?)

US President Donald Trump rn has the most followers on the platform.

## The Details

The company behind the app hopes it will create a steady new source of money for the firm which is rn loss-making. It is likely to be aimed at financial traders who want to see market-moving news fast.

Trump’s socials posts often cause sudden swings in global markets, especially when he writes about trade and tariffs. For firms, a delay of even seconds can be costly.

## Why This Matters

Until now, banks and traders had to monitor the app manually. The new system will send posts directly to paying clients. "Markets already move on Truth Social posts," dropped Kevin McGurn, the interim boss of Trump Media, adding that the service will create a steady profit.

Tech companies have been making moves like this as competition heats up.

## Key Takeaways

- Trump Media did not say how much it would charge for the service.
- The new commercial data feed, named Truth API, promises to deliver posts to paying institutional clients in "milliseconds".
- The service will run 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
- The company, which shipped its socials app in 2022, dropped some firms have been copying its data for months without permission.

## The Bottom Line

Since his family remains the majority holder in the company, the president stands to profit directly from selling expedited access to his own public statements. The BBC has contacted Trump Media for comment on whether or not the presidents posts will be included in the paid feed.
