## The Details

All of the Amazon Kindle models are intentionally designed to be straightforward to use. Grab your Kindle, tap the power button, and youre back reading from the place you left off (its almost as simple as opening a real book).

But, this ease-of-use doesnt mean there arent settings to consider changing. The tweaks can give you a reading experience thats more personalized, enjoyable, and useful.
