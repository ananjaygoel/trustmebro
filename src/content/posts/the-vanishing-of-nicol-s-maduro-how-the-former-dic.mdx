Five months after US special forces toppled Maduro , his heavily curated cult of personality is collapsing, as the deposed president is being airbrushed out of Venezuelan history who seem desperate to move on.

## Why This Matters

Giant images of Maduro and his also incarcerated wife, Cilia Flores, still adorn the capital’s main arteries, some stamped with the hashtag #WeWantThemBackNow. A count-up clock has been erected in the historic centre, logging the number of days since the couple’s kidnapping.
