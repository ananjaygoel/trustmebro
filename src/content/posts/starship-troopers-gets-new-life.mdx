Imagine a game where you're not just fighting bugs, but also trying to survive in a harsh alien environment. That's what Peter Maurice, Director of Starship Troopers: Extermination, is working on. ## What's happening Maurice and his team at Offworld are exploring new types of missions and enemy encounters for the game. This means players can expect a fresh experience with each playthrough. ## Why it matters The original Starship Troopers game was all about mindless action, but Maurice wants to add depth to the gameplay. By introducing new mission types and enemies, he's hoping to attract both old and new fans of the franchise. ## The bottom line With these changes, Starship Troopers: Extermination could become more than just a simple shooter. It could be a game that challenges players to think strategically and work together to survive. But will it be enough to revive the franchise?
