## The Details

In 1979, Gene Roddenberry's sci-fi creation warped beyond the confines of television syndication when Star Trek: The Motion Picture premiered in movie theaters. Star Trek officially became a movie franchise, with 13 more films following theatrically and on streaming.

The first six Star Trek movies centered on Captain James T. Kirk (William Shatner), Spock (Leonard Nimoy), and the Starship Enterprise crew of Star Trek: The Original Series .

## Why This Matters

In 1994, Shatner's Kirk passed the torch to Captain Jean-Luc Picard (Patrick Stewart) and the cast of Star Trek: The Next Generation , which headlined the next four feature films. In 2009, Star Trek movies passed their first solid test with flying colors. Abrams' Star Trek (2009) rebooted the Starship Enterprise's voyages in an alternate reality.

## The Bottom Line

Crucially, Abrams' brilliant recasting proved that the original Starship Enterprise crew could be successfully embodied younger actors, with Chris Pine as Captain Kirk and Zachary Quinto as Spock leading the new cast. Abrams produced a trilogy of Star Trek movies from 2009 to 2016, but a decade later, Paramount has been unable to launch a new theatrical feature film.
