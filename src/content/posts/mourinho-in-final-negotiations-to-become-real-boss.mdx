## What's Happening

Here's the thing: Jose Mourinho is in final negotiations to become Real Madrid's next head coach, 13 years after his first spell at the Bernabeu.

He would replace current head coach Alvaro Arbeloa, who only took charge in January following Xabi Alonsos departure.

## The Details

Real Madrid president Florentino Perez first considered the possibility of Mourinhos return two days after Xabi Alonso left the club, during initial conversations with the Portuguese coachs representatives. Mourinho has been manager of Benfica, in his native Portugal, since joining on a two-year contract last September, and only yesterday told media he did not want to talk about his future just yet.

"Theres a match against Estoril, and from Monday onwards Ill be able to answer questions about my future as a coach and Benficas future," he dropped. Saturdays game against Estoril Praia is Benficas last of the season.
