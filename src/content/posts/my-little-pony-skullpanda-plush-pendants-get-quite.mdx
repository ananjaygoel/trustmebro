## Why This Matters

I know that for these sorts of things, there are rumors about shaking the boxes or trying to judge the weight to tell what you can get. When I held this box, it didn’t fr shuffle at all. Also, while Rarity does feature a prominent horn coming out of her forehead, I couldn’t “feel” it through any of the sides of the box before opening.

Gaming fans have strong opinions about moves like this, and for good reason.

## Key Takeaways

- While I got Rarity, the other five “common” characters are Applejack, Fluttershy, Pinkie Pie, Rainbow Dash, and Twilight Sparkle.
- The two secrets this time are Sunset Shimmer and Queen Chrysalis.
- Once you do open it up, it arrives with a bit more packing to it than other blind box figures.

## The Bottom Line

Once you open that up, additional precautions include a plastic “veil” covering the head and face plate and a hair net covering the hair on the back of the plush pendant’s head. Considering how much it cost, I appreciated to see that extra effort to ensure it’d stay safe while in transit.
