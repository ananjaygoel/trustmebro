## Why This Matters

There are some real positives to this concept. The patent — first spotted by Cheat Happens — emphasizes that the buttons can alter their hardness based on personal preference or gameplay. If you use an elbow, the button might soften so it requires less force to actuate, or harden to prevent accidental misclicks from a palm resting on it, making the DualSense far more accessible to disabled gamers.
