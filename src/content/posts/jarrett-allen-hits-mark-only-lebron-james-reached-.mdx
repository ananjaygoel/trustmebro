## The Details

The centre erupted for 14 points in the third quarter, swinging momentum firmly in Cleveland’s favour. His performance not only proved decisive on the night but also earned a place in franchise history.

## Why This Matters

The only other to achieve that feat was LeBron James. The 28-year-old center finished with 22 points, matching Donovan Mitchell as the team’s top scorer. He also dominated the glass with 19 rebounds, while adding three blocks and two steals.
