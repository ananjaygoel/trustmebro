## What's Happening

Alright so Embark are continuing their meteorological experiments in extraction shooter Arc Raiders with a rousing injection of wind.

There s a hurricane coming to the game on February 24th, a new map condition that limits visibility, degrades shields, impedes movement, and makes you suck even more at shooting things. Still, this stormcloud has a few silver linings. (shocking, we know)

With the gale at your back, you ll naturally move a lot faster.
