Arc Raiders just dropped a patch that's all about shutting down exploits, including that pesky clipping-through-locked-rooms trick players have been using to bypass mechanics.

## What's happening The patch, which went live on the game's servers, addresses several key issues that have been plaguing the game. For instance, players were able to clip through locked rooms, giving them access to areas they shouldn't have been able to reach. This not only gave them an unfair advantage but also broke the game's intended flow. The developers have been working hard to identify and fix these exploits, and this patch is the result of their efforts. With this update, players will no longer be able to use these exploits to gain an unfair advantage, and the game should be more balanced as a result.

## Why it matters So, why does this matter? Well, for one, it shows that the developers are committed to keeping the game fair and balanced. By fixing these exploits, they're ensuring that all players have an equal chance of succeeding, regardless of their skill level or experience. It also highlights the importance of community feedback in game development. The fact that players were able to identify and report these exploits shows that the community is engaged and willing to help improve the game.
