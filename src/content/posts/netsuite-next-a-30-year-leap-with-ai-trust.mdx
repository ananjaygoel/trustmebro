## What's Happening Evan Goldberg, founder and EVP of Oracle NetSuite and a pioneer in cloud computing, made a huge announcement at SuiteWorld 2025. He declared NetSuite Next as the company's most significant product evolution in nearly three decades. This isn't just about new features; it marks a profound shift. The core of this evolution centers on how AI behaves, focusing on "engineering trust into AI" to create a transparent, "glass box" approach for users. ## Why This Matters A product release deemed the biggest in almost 30 years signals a monumental re-imagining of NetSuite's capabilities. It promises to fundamentally reshape how businesses interact with their cloud ERP systems. The emphasis on trusted AI is particularly vital in today's data-driven landscape. As AI becomes integral to business operations, users need assurance that its insights are reliable, ethical, and understandable. This commitment to building trustworthy AI will have several key impacts:
- It aims to boost user confidence in AI-driven recommendations and automated processes.
- Businesses can expect greater transparency, understanding how AI arrives at its conclusions.
- It seeks to minimize risks associated with AI biases or errors, leading to more accurate and dependable outcomes.  ## The Bottom Line NetSuite Next represents a significant leap forward, not just in technology, but in fostering a new era of confidence in enterprise AI.  It poses a crucial question for the industry: Can transparency truly become the default for artificial intelligence?
