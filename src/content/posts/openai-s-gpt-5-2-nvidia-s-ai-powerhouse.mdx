This significant launch marks another leap in AI capabilities. Crucially, this advanced model was both trained and deployed on NVIDIA's extensive infrastructure. Specifically, NVIDIA Hopper and GB200 NVL72 systems were utilized, showcasing their full-stack AI capabilities. ## Why This Matters This isn't just about a new AI model; it's a testament to NVIDIA's foundational role in the rapidly evolving AI landscape.
