## The Bottom Line

Their front four is frightening (with Desire Doue or Bradley Barcola), Kylian Mbappes the happiest I have ever seen him at a major tournament, William Saliba is one of the best defenders at the tournament, unsung Adrien Rabiot links things brilliantly and there is also so much strength in depth. I saw them live twice, in New Jersey and Philadelphia, and I would be stunned if they do not make it to the final.
