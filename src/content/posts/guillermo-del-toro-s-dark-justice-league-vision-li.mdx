Even years after the project was shelved, del Toro revealed his deep affection for the script he penned remains unwavering. His passion for bringing characters like John Constantine and Zatanna to the big screen in his unique style is still palpable. ## Why This Matters Del Toro's involvement would have brought his signature blend of gothic horror, intricate creature design, and profound character empathy to the DC universe.
