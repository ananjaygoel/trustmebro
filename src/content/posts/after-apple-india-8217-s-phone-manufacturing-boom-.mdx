## What's Happening

So get this: Vivo's joint venture could become a template for Chinese phone makers in India.

India on Thursday approved a manufacturing joint venture between Chinas Vivo and local manufacturer Dixon Technologies, a move that could mark the next phase of the countrys phone manufacturing boom after Apple helped turn India into a global phone production hub. (shocking, we know)

The approval allows Vivo to proceed with a long-delayed manufacturing partnership first just dropped in December 2024, after New Delhi cleared the investment under investment rules introduced in 2020 that require extra government scrutiny of investment from countries sharing a land border with India — a category that includes China.
