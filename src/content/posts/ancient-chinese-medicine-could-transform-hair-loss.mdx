## What's Happening

Breaking it down: A traditional Chinese medicinal root used for over a thousand years is attracting new scientific attention for its potential to combat hair loss.

Studies suggest Polygonum multiflorum can block harmful hormones, activate hair-growth signals, protect follicles, and boost blood flow to the scalp. (wild, right?)

Researchers say the herb’s effects align remarkably well with both ancient descriptions and modern hair biology.
