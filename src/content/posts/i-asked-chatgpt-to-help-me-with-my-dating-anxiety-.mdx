## Why This Matters

Answer whatever feels easy: What’s been hardest lately: meeting women, getting dates, or keeping momentum? Where do you usually meet women (apps, friends, work, hobbies)? How do you feel on dates—confident, nervous, unsure what to say?

## Key Takeaways

- What kind of relationship are you actually looking for?
- What feedback (if any) have you gotten after dates?
- How’s your social life overall?
- What do you like about yourself as a partner?
