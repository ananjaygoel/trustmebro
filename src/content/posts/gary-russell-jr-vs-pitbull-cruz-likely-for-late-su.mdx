## What's Happening

Breaking it down: Gary Antuanne Russell and Isaac “Pitbull” Cruz are likely to fight in August or September, according to ESPN Knockout’s Salvador Rodriguez.

Rodriguez says that August 22 and September 5 are possible dates. The fight is targeted for Las Vegas. (yes, really)

## The Details

The 29-year-old Russell fought on February 21, holding off a solid challenge from Andy Hiraoka to retain the title, which he clutched from Jose Valenzuela in 2025. If this fight does happen in 2026, it would be the first time since 2022 that Russell has fought twice in a calendar year.

Cruz, 28, is coming off of a draw with Lamont Roach Jr last December. Many observers felt Roach probably deserved the win that night, but two judges scored it 113-113, and the third scored for Cruz, 115-111.

## Why This Matters

Cruz did drop Roach in the third round. Russell vs Cruz could be a terrific fight, as Cruz is a fearless, come-forward fighter, and at his best, Russell is a strong technician with some good pop and timing. It could bring the best out of both men for entertainment.
