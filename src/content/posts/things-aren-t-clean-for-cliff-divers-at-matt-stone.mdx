Plus to a safer environment to perform stunts that have already resulted in concussions, hypothermia, chlorine toxicity, and sexual harassment from patrons, the workers would also like to make as much money as the servers. The staff organized in April 2024, unionizing under Actors Equity.

Per CNN , there have been 14 bargaining sessions, none of which were attended Stone and Parker, who rn stars in Minions & Monsters (Its just been silence, radio silence, says Shields of the South Park guys). Shields tells CNN that the union has made concessions that have yielded a pay raise of less than a dollar an hour.
