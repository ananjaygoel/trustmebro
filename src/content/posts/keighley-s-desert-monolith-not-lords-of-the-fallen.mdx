This enigmatic monolith is teasing an upcoming game announcement, but its identity remains shrouded in mystery. Adding fuel to the speculative fire, CI Games CEO Marek Tyminski has officially debunked one of the most popular theories. He confirmed that the impressive desert installation is definitively *not* promoting Lords of the Fallen 2, narrowing down the possibilities for eager fans. ## Why This Matters This isn't just a random statue; it's a meticulously crafted marketing spectacle, a hallmark of Geoff Keighley's approach to game announcements.
