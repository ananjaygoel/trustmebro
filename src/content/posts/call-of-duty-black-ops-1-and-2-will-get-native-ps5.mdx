## What's Happening

So get this: PS3 shooters ported to both PS5 and PS4.

Activision’s just just dropped Call of Duty: Black Ops ports will be available across PS5 and PS4, as locked in by new Trophy lists on Sony’s servers. The publisher spilled its intent to bring these classic first-person shooters to contemporary consoles last month via dev Iron Galaxy, but it wasn’t clear whether it’d rely on PS4 backwards compatibility to serve both systems. ## Why This Matters

Activision’s dropped the games will release in July, and the emergence of these Trophies suggests a date is imminent. It hasn’t locked in pricing yet, but recent speculation pegs them at $40 apiece – without any DLC included.
