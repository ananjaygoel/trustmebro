## The Details

What could have been the end of a dream became the foundation for a remarkable journey. Inside their modest home in Punjabi Bagh, Arnav trains in a narrow corridor turned into a makeshift net.

At the other end, seated in a wheelchair, his mother Kusum Lata, his first coach, acts as his bowling partner, providing an unwavering support system. She bowls to him every day, not out of convenience, but out of commitment.
