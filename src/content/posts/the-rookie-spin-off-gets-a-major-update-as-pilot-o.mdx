## The Details

He has mentioned that, while he’d like to move on at some point, he has at least 10 more years of playing John Nolan left in him. With any show as popular as The Rookie , which has been on the air for eight seasons, it’s only natural for the fandom to start asking about spin-offs.

The Rookie had its first spin-off back in 2022, The Rookie: Feds , but it was canceled after only one season. The show followed Special Agent Simone Clarke ( Niecy Nash ), the oldest rookie in the FBI Academy.

## Why This Matters

Just, ABC locked in that another spin-off is in the works. The Rookie: North will follow a middle-aged man who joins the Pierce County Police Department as its oldest rookie after a traumatic home invasion. One of the names attached to star in The Rookie: North is Karen Fukuhara , who has earned acclaim in the last seven years for her performance as Kimiko in the hit Prime Video series, The Boys .
