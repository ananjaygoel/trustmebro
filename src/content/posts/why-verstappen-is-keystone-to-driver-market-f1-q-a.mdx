## Why This Matters

The four-time champion has a contract with Red Bull that runs until 2028. But, it has performance clauses in it, which mean he is free to leave the team if he is not in a certain position in the championship by a certain point of the season. The way Red Bull are performing at the moment, there is absolutely no chance that Verstappen will be high enough in the championship to lock him in break unless they have a massive turnaround in form.
