## What's Happening

A gamer, whose Reddit post quickly went viral, has finally reunited with their extensive collection after a decade-long hiatus.  This treasure trove of classic consoles and games was held in storage, sparking a wave of nostalgia across the internet.

The individual recently returned to the UK after living abroad for four years, making this reunion possible.  The '10 years in storage' period highlights a significant wait, underscoring the anticipation for rediscovering these beloved items.

The shared image reveals an impressive haul: iconic systems like the PlayStation 2, the original Xbox, a Nintendo GameCube, and even older gems such as the Nintendo 64 and PlayStation 1.  The collection also includes handhelds like a Game Boy, alongside a stack of classic game titles ready for action.
