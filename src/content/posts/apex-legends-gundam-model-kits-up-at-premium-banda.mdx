## What's Happening

Not gonna lie, Bandai Namco opened pre-orders for the three gunpla model kits that are part of the Apex Legends Gundam crossover event.

There are three styles that range in price between $49 and $65. All are expected to ship in September 2026, though there’s no exact date for their debut yet. (yes, really)

As before just dropped, these are all RG 1/144 kits.

## The Details

They will all be about five to six inches tall when finished. They will be articulated and all come with weapons.
