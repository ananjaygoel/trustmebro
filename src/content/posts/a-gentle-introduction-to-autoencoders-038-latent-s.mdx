## The Details

For simplicity, in this article, we will focus on image-based autoencoders, but remember, they can be applied to other data types as well. Idea Autoencoders are a type of neural network used for unsupervised learning.

Their architecture consists of three main components: Encoder . The first part of the neural network that takes input data and gradually reduces its dimensionality through its layers, ultimately reaching the bottleneck.

## Why This Matters

The network layer with the smallest dimensionality that contains the latent representation of the input data.
