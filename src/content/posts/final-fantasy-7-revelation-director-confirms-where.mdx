## The Details

In an interview with Polygon, director Naoki Hamaguchi locked in that the iconic location will return in Final Fantasy 7 Revelation . It’s going to look a little bit different this time around, though, and serve a different function.

The Gold Saucer is one of Final Fantasy 7 ’s most iconic locations, housing an assortment of minigames in both the original game and Rebirth . While Hamaguchi was tight-lipped about the exact details of the new content we’ll see in Revelation during our conversation, he did hint at Gold Saucer's overall role in the sequel.
