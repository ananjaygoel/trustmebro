## Why This Matters

Safety regulations at US venues mean play is suspended when lightning is recorded within roughly 8 miles of a stadium, not resuming until 30 minutes after the last strike. The risk of thunderstorms is greatest across the central, eastern and south-eastern US. Among the host cities, Miami and Houston are the most lightning-prone, although Houston’s stadium has a retractable roof.

## The Bottom Line

The greatest risk of severe storms over the coming days, but, lies further north-east: scattered storms capable of strong gusts and isolated large hail are possible across the north-eastern US and south-eastern Canada , placing Friday’s Canada v Bosnia and Herzegovina match in Toronto and Saturday’s Boston and New York fixtures at slight risk. The weather hazards likely to pose the broadest challenge to players and spectators are heat and humidity.
