## The Details

The best foot baths allow you to soak your tired toes, with extra features like bubbles, jets, and massaging features to help your feet feel rejuvenated. A good foot soak at the end of a long day or workout can do wonders for your mental health, too, ultimately supporting your overall health.

But unsurprisingly, a lot of the potential perks from home foot baths revolve around your feet. These baths can help boost circulation in your feet and combat inflammation that could lead to pain, swelling, and overuse injuries if it’s left unchecked, says Bradley Schaeffer, DPM, a podiatrist and foot surgeon specializing in sports medicine in New York City.
