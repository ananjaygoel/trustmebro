This news confirms her departure from her iconic role as Black Widow in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Her involvement marks a significant shift for the acclaimed actress, signaling a new chapter in her superhero film career. She's now stepping into the gritty, grounded world crafted by Matt Reeves, leaving behind the Avengers for Gotham City. ## Why This Matters This casting is a massive win for DC and Matt Reeves, demonstrating their ability to attract A-list talent from rival franchises. It immediately elevates the profile of *The Batman 2* and adds considerable buzz to the project. For Johansson, this move represents a bold step beyond her hugely successful, long-running MCU tenure. It offers her a fresh opportunity to tackle a new, potentially complex character within an established, critically praised universe. - Major talent acquisition for DC, boosting their cinematic universe's prestige.
