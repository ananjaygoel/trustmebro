## What's Happening

Listen up: A major review of 217 trials shows that aerobic exercise is the most effective option for managing knee osteoarthritis.

Activities like walking, cycling, and swimming outperformed other exercise types in reducing pain and improving movement. (shocking, we know)

While alternatives like strength training and mind-body exercises help, they are best used alongside aerobic workouts.
