Not only is a brand-new game on the way this March, but the latest news is that the Life is Strange TV adaptation from Prime Video has found the immaculate actors to play Max and Chloe.

## The Details

Tatum Grace Hopkins ( Queen of Versailles ) will take on the awkward mantle of shy geek Max Caulfield, with Maisy Stella ( My Old Ass ) stepping into the spiked boots of troubled punk, Chloe Price. Hopkins celebrated the announcement on Instagram, wearing a shirt that eagle-eyed Life is Strange the fandom instantly recognized as a reference .

The adaptation is based on the critically acclaimed video game franchise from Dontnod, which follows photography student Max, who discovers she can rewind time after witnessing a tragic incident involving her childhood best friend, Chloe. The pair team up to try to understand Max's new powers, all while investigating the mysterious disappearance of Rachel Amber.

## Why This Matters

Their investigation leads them to uncover a dark side of Arcadia Bay, one that forces them to make an impossible choice.
