## What's Happening

Here's the thing: There’s a new Japanese release date for Puella Magi Madoka Magica the Movie - Walpurgisnacht: Rising, and the film will apparently appear in theaters in Japan on August 28, 2026.

There’s no worldwide release window yet. The news arrived via the official site, and there’s no new trailer to accompany it. (shocking, we know)

But, Good Smile company did share an update on the Homura Akemi, Kyoko Sakura, and Sayaka Miki Nendoroids based on their appearances in this release, rather than their original anime looks.
