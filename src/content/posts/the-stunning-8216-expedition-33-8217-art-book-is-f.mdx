## What's Happening

So get this: The story of 'Expedition 33' may be over, but its insane art is still here for you to pore through and put on your bookshelf.

Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 was one of 2025s biggest and best games, and players fell in love with it. For the fandom of the games art style, a book collecting it all will soon hit North America for your viewing pleasure. (and honestly, same)

Publishers UDON and Pix’n Love have teamed up for a new version of the games art book.

## The Details

Its been adapted into English after before being just in French, and will come in standard and collectors editions. Both feature concept art, design notes, and developer insights from Sandfall, but the $100 collectors edition has some extra add-ons.

These goodies include a golden cardboard slipcase in the colors of Alicias mask, prints of the games cast on textured paper, and a cardboard portfolio with exclusive art. This exclusive large-format hardcover volume gathers, across more than 300 pages, hundreds of before unseen documents and illustrations used in the creation of one of the most fascinating games of recent years, reads the description.

## Why This Matters
