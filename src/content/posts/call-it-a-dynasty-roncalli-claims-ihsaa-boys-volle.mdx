## The Details

Against a familiar opponent on Indiana's biggest stage, they showed exactly why. Roncalli swept Cathedral 25-21, 25-16, 25-13 Saturday evening at Lawrence North High School to capture the second sanctioned boys volleyball state championship in Indiana High School Athletic Association history.
