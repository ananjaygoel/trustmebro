The kind of performance you were used to starts becoming a memory, and you're forced to compromise on the graphics settings to extend your GPU's lifespan . That dropped, raw performance is just one way your GPU feels outdated.

Depending on its age, it probably has way bigger limitations than sub-60 FPS. The factors that make your old GPU "too old" come down to its architecture, hardware limitations, software limitations, and memory.
