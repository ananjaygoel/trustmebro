## What's Happening Bandai Namco just announced a new Grimmjow Jaegerjaquez figure for their Banpresto Maximatic line. This highly anticipated collectible brings the iconic Sexta Espada to your shelf. It will first appear as a prize figure in Japanese arcades starting January 2026. Global fans can expect it to launch on Amazon US by April 11, 2026, priced at an accessible $35. ## Why This Matters This release is a big deal for Bleach enthusiasts, especially those who collect figures. The Maximatic line is known for its dynamic, active poses, promising a visually striking representation of Grimmjow. The $35 price point makes this high-quality figure incredibly accessible, not just for dedicated collectors but also for casual fans. Its confirmed worldwide availability ensures everyone gets a fair shot at owning a piece of the Bleach universe. ## The Bottom Line With a detailed, dynamic pose and an attractive price point, the Maximatic Grimmjow figure is shaping up to be a must-have for any Bleach fan.
