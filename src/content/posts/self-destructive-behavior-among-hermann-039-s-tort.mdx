## What's Happening

Not gonna lie, On the strictly protected island of Golem Grad in North Macedonia, the tortoises are destroying their own population.

During prolonged courtship, aggressive males are exhausting the females and frequently pushing them off the cliffs. (let that sink in)

So basically, there are now one hundred males for every female capable of laying eggs.
