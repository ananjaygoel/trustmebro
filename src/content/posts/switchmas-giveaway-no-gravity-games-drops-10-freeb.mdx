## What's Happening No Gravity Games is bringing back its annual 'Switchmas Giveaway', a festive tradition where they dish out a selection of their Nintendo Switch back catalogue for free. This year, players can snag a total of 10 free games, just in time for the holidays. The giveaway kicks off on December 12th, offering one free game daily until December 18th. To cap it all off, a generous three more freebies will drop on December 19th, with 'One True Hero' confirmed as part of the lineup. ## Why This Matters This yearly event is a fantastic opportunity for Nintendo Switch owners to expand their game library without spending a dime. It's a perfect way to discover new titles or genres you might not have tried otherwise, especially during the expensive festive season. For No Gravity Games, it's a smart move to engage their community and introduce more players to their diverse portfolio. Giving away older titles can generate buzz, encourage new purchases, and build brand loyalty among Switch fans. - Players get access to a range of new games for free.
- It's a chance to explore different game styles and developers.
- Gamers save money during a typically expensive time of year.  ## The Bottom Line The 'Switchmas Giveaway' is a win-win, offering generous freebies to players while boosting visibility for No Gravity Games.  Are you ready to claim your free Switch games this holiday season?
