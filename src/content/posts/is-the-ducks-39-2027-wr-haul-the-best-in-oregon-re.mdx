## The Details

Across the board, Lanning has been able to find elite players to fill out his roster. But, it's been wild to see how good the recruiting has been, particularly at the wide receiver position, where Oregon has landed a 5-star in each of the last five recruiting classes.

For an Oregon program that had just one single 5-star WR — Cameron Colvin in 2003 — before Lanning took over, that's wild. This year alone, the Ducks have commitments from a pair of 5-star receivers, with WR Xavier Sabb joining WR Dakota Guerrant this past week with his pledge.
