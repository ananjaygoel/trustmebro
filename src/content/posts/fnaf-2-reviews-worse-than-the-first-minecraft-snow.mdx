## What's Happening The highly anticipated sequel, *Five Nights At Freddy's 2*, is reportedly hitting rock bottom with critics. Kotaku just dropped the bombshell that reviews for the new film are 'even worse than you think.' Apparently, the horror flick manages to be worse than its already divisive predecessor. Shockingly, it's also being panned as inferior to even *Minecraft* and *Snow White*. ## Why This Matters This isn't just a bad movie review; it's a significant blow to a hugely popular franchise. Fans, who flocked to the first film despite its mixed reception, might be hesitant for the sequel. The consistent failure of video game adaptations to capture critical acclaim continues. It raises questions about Hollywood's approach to translating beloved game narratives. The poor reception could have several ripple effects:
- It might jeopardize future installments in the *Five Nights At Freddy's* cinematic universe, despite the first film's box office success.
- This further fuels the stereotype that video game movies are inherently flawed, making it harder for quality adaptations to gain traction.
- It could erode audience trust, making fans wary of upcoming projects from Blumhouse and Universal, even outside the FNAF series.  ## The Bottom Line Ultimately, *Five Nights At Freddy's 2* seems destined to join the pantheon of critically lambasted video game films.  The big question now is, will audiences still show up, or have critics finally scared them away?
