In a study published in the journal Earth and Planetary Science Letters , astronomers say the chunk of space rock known as Northwest Africa (NWA) 12774 once belonged to a protoplanet possibly as large as Mars . That is, until a cosmic crash likely blew it to smithereens.

## Why This Matters

The solar system includes eight known planets (sorry, Pluto ). Barring interstellar catastrophe, this number will remain the same until the sun finally dies about 5 billion years from now. But, this total planetary count was never a guarantee.
