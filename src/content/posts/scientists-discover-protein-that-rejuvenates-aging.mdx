## What's Happening

Here's the thing: A newly identified protein may hold the key to rejuvenating aging brain cells.

Researchers found that boosting DMTF1 can restore the ability of neural stem cells to regenerate, even when age-related damage has set in. (let that sink in)

Without it, these cells struggle to renew and support memory and learning.
