After a turbulent road to the World Cup, Iran finally got to just play soccer, playing New Zealand to a 2-2 draw before 70,108 the fandom at SoFi Stadium. Iran twice rallied from behind against a lower-ranked but fearless New Zealand side, salvaging a draw that kept all four Group G teams level on points.
