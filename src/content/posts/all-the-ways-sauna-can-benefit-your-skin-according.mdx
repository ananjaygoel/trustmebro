## What's Happening

Here's the thing: Turns out, turning up the heat and humidity can be a major plus for your complexion.

Name a relaxation practice, I’ve tried it. Yoga , meditation , journaling , box breathing…the list goest on and on. (shocking, we know)

But nothing has gotten my frenetic Type A-self to actually chill out quite as much as sauna .

## The Details

I kicked off going regularly a little over a year ago and was ASAP blown away calmer it made me. And while my mind was Zen-ed out, my skin was glowing .

I’d leave sweaty, yes, but also looking refreshed and radiant. Placebo effect or legit benefit?

## Why This Matters

Turns out, it may well be the latter. “Patients ask me all the time about the sauna and I tell them it’s like a power walk for your complexion—a quick way to give your skin a little boost,” says Mona Gohara , MD, clinical professor of dermatology at Yale School of Medicine. The high level: Heat increases circulation, bringing more blood and oxygen to your skin, there appearance.

Medical professionals are taking note of this development.
