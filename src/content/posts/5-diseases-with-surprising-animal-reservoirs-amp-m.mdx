## Why This Matters

“Every time there is a new pathogen, it comes from a host we didn’t expect,” says Barbara Han, a disease ecologist at the Cary Institute of Ecosystem Studies, a nonprofit research organization. To stay on top of the emergence of such diseases, Han says that studying wildlife health is critical, since animals are the source of many diseases that affect humans . Otherwise, figuring out where an emerging disease came from is difficult.
