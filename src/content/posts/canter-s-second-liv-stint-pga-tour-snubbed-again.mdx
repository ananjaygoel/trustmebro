## What's Happening English golfer Laurie Canter has once again made a significant move, rejecting an opportunity to play on the PGA Tour. This marks the second time Canter has chosen to turn down a PGA Tour card. Instead of joining the established tour, Canter has decided to rejoin the Saudi-backed LIV Golf circuit. His decision firmly places him back with the breakaway league. ## Why This Matters Canter's repeated choice highlights the ongoing magnetic pull of LIV Golf for some players, despite efforts towards potential unification in the sport. It signals that the financial incentives and different format offered by LIV remain highly attractive. This move also serves as a clear indication that the battle for talent between the PGA Tour and LIV Golf is far from over. Players are still making choices that shape the future landscape of professional golf. - It underscores the continuing appeal of LIV Golf's model, likely due to its lucrative contracts and lighter schedule.
