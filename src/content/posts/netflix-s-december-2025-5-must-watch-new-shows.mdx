## What's Happening December 2025 is almost here, and Netflix is wrapping up the year with a bang. We've got the inside track on five brand-new shows hitting your screens, perfect for holiday binge-watching. From gritty dramas to animated adventures, there's truly something for everyone in this final lineup. Trust us, you'll want to clear your schedule for these. Here’s a sneak peek at what’s cooking for December 2025: - **Crimson Tides (Release: December 6, 2025):** A dark, brooding detective series set in a rain-swept coastal town. Detective Eva Rostova hunts a serial killer whose victims are tied to ancient maritime legends.
- **Chronos Echoes (Release: December 13, 2025):** This mind-bending sci-fi thriller explores the consequences of altering the past.  A rogue scientist races against time to fix a fractured timeline before reality unravels completely.
- **Suburban Surreal (Release: December 18, 2025):** A quirky dark comedy about the Peterson family, whose mundane suburban life gets repeatedly disrupted by increasingly bizarre, supernatural events.  Think 'Arrested Development' meets 'Twilight Zone.
- **The Culinary Gauntlet (Release: December 20, 2025):** A high-stakes reality cooking competition where amateur chefs battle it out in extreme environments.  Hosted by celebrity chef Gordon Flamet, expect intense pressure and unexpected twists.
- **Aisha's Astral Adventures (Release: December 25, 2025):** A vibrant animated series for all ages.
