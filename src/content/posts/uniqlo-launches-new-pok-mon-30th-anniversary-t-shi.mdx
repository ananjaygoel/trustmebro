## What's Happening

So get this: As part of Pokémon's ongoing 30th anniversary celebrations, Uniqlo has shipped its second UT collection of t-shirt designs.

According to the official description, this collection pays homage to the origins of the Pokémon series and comes with game pixel art dating back to the original generation. There are four shirts for adults and four available in kid sizes. (it feels like chaos)
