## The Details

The news comes straight from the Xbox blog , which states: "Discord Nitro now includes a starter edition of Xbox Game Pass where available, giving eligible members a new way to discover and play solid games. " Note the deal only applies to the regular $9.

"Discord has also rolled out updates that make it even easier to discover and jump into games included with Game Pass," the blog added. This means there's a proper integration now that allows players to instantly try the games included in the Game Pass sub if their friends show that activity on Discord, so it's only a couple of clicks (and a download) away.

## Why This Matters

The full announcement by Discord's team confirms this version of Game Pass comes with a 10-hour trial of cloud gaming and games like Fallout 4 , Stardew Valley, DayZ , Deep Rock Galactic, Overcooked 2 , Grounded, "and more". Xbox blockbusters like Gears 5 , Doom Eternal, and Dishonored 2 are apparently included too. There's also the promise of more games being added "periodically", but it's unclear how long they'll stay on the service for.
