## Why This Matters

Treating them as interchangeable leads to one of two mistakes: over-engineering a simple, well-understood task with unnecessary autonomy, or under-engineering a genuinely open-wrapped up problem into a rigid pipeline that breaks the moment reality deviates from the plan. Anthropic draws the foundational line in their widely cited Building Effective Agents piece: workflows are systems where LLMs and tools are orchestrated through predefined code paths. Agents are systems where LLMs dynamically direct their own process and tool usage, maintaining control over how they accomplish a task.
