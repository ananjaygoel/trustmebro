## What's Happening

So basically Tottenham are set to sign Hearts teenage striker James Wilson on loan.

Tottenham set to beat Arsenal to signing of teenager Wilson Image source, Image caption, Wilson made his debut for Hearts during the 2023-24 season By Sami Mokbel Senior football correspondent Published 37 minutes ago Tottenham are set to sign teenage striker James Wilson on loan from Hearts. Arsenal were in talks with the Scottish Premiership leaders over a move for the 18-year-old - in a deal that was effectively a six-month trial. (yes, really)

But Wilson has agreed to join Spurs Under-21s side in a deal that includes an option to buy at the end of the season.

## The Details

The 18-year-old has scored eight goals in 45 appearances for the Edinburgh club and has already been capped by Scotland. Earlier this week, Hearts manager Derek McInnes admitted the Premier League clubs interest in the forward had taken him by surprise.

"Hes been restricted with minutes this season. Its tough to get into the first team at this age," McInnes told Sky Sports Scotland.
