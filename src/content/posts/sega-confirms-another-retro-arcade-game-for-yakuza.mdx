## The Details

Following the recent Game Gear news , another arcade title has been spilled. As noted by our pals at PushSquare , it's the "delightfully oddball" Model 3 release, Magical Truck Adventure .

It will be joined by Emergency Call Ambulance and SlashOut . Image: via Sega Retro This title originally debuted in 1998 and is a solo and co-op "on-the-rails" action experience where you control two kids named Roy and Alma who must power a hand-operated rail cart to retrieve a "magical stone".
