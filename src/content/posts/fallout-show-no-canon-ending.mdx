So, you know how we've all been waiting for the Fallout show to give us some real answers about the Mojave Wasteland and New Vegas?

## The Tea ☕

It's been announced that the Fallout show is heading to the Mojave Wasteland for its second season, which is lowkey a whole thing, but they're not going to give us a canon ending for New Vegas.

Like, what's the point of even going there if you're not going to tie up some loose ends?

It's giving 'we don't want to upset the fans' vibes, but also 'we don't care about your feelings' at the same time.
