## The Details

The pirates departed a remote coastal area near the seaside town of Qandala, which sits on the Gulf of Aden, according to three separate security officials from the semi-autonomous Puntland region who spoke with the BBC. It marks the second hijacking of an oil tanker in the area in a 10-day period, following the hijacking of Honor 25 on April 22.

Honor 25 was carrying 18,500 barrels of oil bound for Mogadishu. MT Eureka was sailing on the flag of the west African nation of Togo prior to the hijacking and was overrun at 5:00 AM local time (03:00 BST) this morning, the security official went on to tell the BBC.
