## What's Happening Forget dusty blueprints and endless spreadsheets. New research shows construction contractors are now strongly embracing AI to revolutionize their industry. This insight comes from a joint study by Dodge Construction Network (Dodge) and CMiC, highlighting a significant shift in how projects are managed. The construction sector is a data-generating powerhouse, but much of this information often goes unused or gets trapped in traditional formats. AI is stepping in to unlock this potential, transforming raw data into actionable intelligence. ## Why This Matters This isn't just about buzzwords; it’s about concrete improvements for the entire industry. By leveraging AI, teams can finally tap into the colossal amounts of data generated daily, moving away from guesswork. The true transformative power, according to contractors, lies in several key areas:
- Accelerating decision-making processes
- Enhancing project margins and profitability
- Improving overall project outcomes and efficiency ## The Bottom Line The message is clear: AI is no longer a futuristic concept for construction but a present-day tool driving significant change.
