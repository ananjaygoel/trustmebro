## What's Happening

So basically Surviving cancer at a young age may come with an unexpected cost: faster aging at both the cellular and brain levels.

Researchers found that survivors often show signs of being biologically older than their actual age, with chemotherapy accelerating the process most dramatically. (yes, really)

This accelerated aging is linked to struggles with memory and focus, which can ripple into education and career outcomes.
