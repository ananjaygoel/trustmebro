## Why This Matters

The analysis looked at the period since UN resolution 2417 unanimously condemned the deliberate starvation of civilians in 2018 . It found starvation is being increasingly weaponised with the supply of food routinely targeted in Gaza, Sudan, Lebanon and Haiti among others. Data compiled by Insecurity Insight uncovered 21,403 incidents in 15 countries where food supplies have been deliberately targeted since 2018, when the UN security council unanimously passed a resolution condemning the unlawful denial of humanitarian aid as a tactic of warfare.

Diplomats and experts are analyzing what this means for international relations.

## The Bottom Line

Researchers found out 1,909 military strikes on farmland, and another 563 on water infrastructure vital for crops, which affected food security in more than 42 countries and territories.
