## What's Happening

Listen up: Arc Raiders has a new Headwinds game update that, amongst many other things, removes an extravagantly drawn-out puzzle from the game s Buried City map.

Introduced in December, the puzzle in question saw people tracking down and pushing 15 buttons in a certain order to unlock a secret chamber containing an actual, strummable guitar. As of this updating, you ll now be able to buy that guitar at Shani s, like a millionaire tourist paying to be flown to the top of Mount Everest. (wild, right?)
