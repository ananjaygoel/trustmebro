## Crypto Fail: Firm Cancels Elections After Losing Encryption Key

fr fr, it looks like someone didn't understand the assignment 🤦‍♂️. A cryptology firm just cancelled elections after losing the encryption key. Yes, you read that right - the key to secure communication was lost. 

## What Happened, Though?

The International Association for Cryptologic Research (IACR) - created to study, what else, secure communication 🤔 - said it was an 'honest human mistake.' We're guessing it's not the first time they've made a mistake, but it's definitely the most highkey dramatic one. Like, who loses the encryption key? It's not like it's a spare phone charger or something 📱.

## Secure Communication... Not So Secure

According to reports, the IACR was working on a project to create a secure online voting system. But with the encryption key gone, the system is basically as secure as a TikTok account 🤣. The IACR said they're working on a new key, but until then, the elections are cancelled. That's like someone saying they're 'lowkey' working on a new project, but it's really just a bunch of nothing 🙄.
