## What's Happening Ellen McLain, the legendary voice behind Portal's sarcastic AI, GLaDOS, has revealed a surprising personal struggle. Despite her character's digital genius, McLain admits she couldn't even complete the game's very first test chamber. This isn't just a one-off issue; McLain openly shares that she generally struggles with video games. Her inability to solve the initial Portal puzzle highlights a stark contrast to her character's calculating prowess. ## Why This Matters The revelation is a delightful piece of irony for fans, humanizing an actor often associated with a cold, calculating machine. It underscores the unique skill set required for voice acting versus actual gameplay. It also offers a relatable moment for many players who've felt challenged by seemingly simple game mechanics. Even the 'voice of God' in a game can find its puzzles perplexing. - Highlights the distinct skills of voice acting versus gameplay.
