## What's Happening PS Plus Essential December 2025 monthly free games have officially started rolling out to subscribers. The rollout kicked off first in Japan and Asia, where it was already Tuesday, December 2. Members are being treated to a truly 'bumper lineup' this month, a noticeable increase from the usual offerings. This generous package includes a total of five free games, a significant boost that's likely a special, one-off event. ## Why This Matters This unusual bounty of free titles isn't just random generosity; it signals potential shifts within the PS Plus service. The source material highlights this 'bumper lineup' as a likely 'one-off' ahead of significant, 'incoming changes to the service for PS4 users. For PlayStation 4 owners, this could be a bittersweet moment. It strongly suggests that Sony is preparing to pivot its focus, potentially reducing future support or dedicated game offerings for its older console within the Essential tier. This generous December drop might be one of the last major giveaways specifically catering to the PS4 user base. ## The Bottom Line Enjoy these five extra games while they last, especially if you're still primarily a PS4 gamer.
