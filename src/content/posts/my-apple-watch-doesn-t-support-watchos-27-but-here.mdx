If your iPhone is rn getting the latest updates from Apple, it will continue to do so for another year.

## The Details

Unfortunately, some of the company's other products weren't so lucky. As expected, macOS 27 Golden Gate marks the end of the line for Intel Macs .

Unless you have an Apple silicon Mac, you clutched't be able to update this fall. It's a similar story for iPadOS 27: Apple is dropping a number of iPads this year, mostly from 2018 and 2019.

## Why This Matters

But the real shock came with watchOS 27 : Apple's lineup is now limited to just six Apple Watches, which means there's a decent chance your watch isn't backed this year. While the company developed watchOS 26 for the Series 6, 7, 8, first-gen Ultra and second-gen SE, none of those watches will get the new update in the fall.
