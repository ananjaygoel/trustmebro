## What's Happening

Real talk: Researchers have found out that beneficial soil bacteria give plants an unexpected survival advantage in salty soils.

Instead of helping plants keep salt out, the microbes stimulate the production of lignin, a natural compound that strengthens roots and makes plants more resilient. (shocking, we know)

Greenhouse and field tests showed healthier plants and higher yields in salty conditions.
