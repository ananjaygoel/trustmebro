## The Details

But, we can hope that the gap is closing fast. Around the clock, an independent community of researchers and developers is pushing to ensure this technology is accessible to anyone with a computer.

Today, the foundation for true democratization is already here: you can run a highly capable model entirely on your own laptop. For today’s experiment, I set out to find a large language model that can run entirely on my laptop — and use it for the simple tasks I’d normally hand off to a big lab model.

## Why This Matters

We’ll install Qwen 3 8B on my MacBook Air, run it fully offline, and finally have a language model living on my own machine instead of a distant datacenter. The Qwen family of models have been trained by Alibaba (the chinese company) and are fully open source, available on the the internet for everyone to download. The model has 9 billion weights and takes up around 6gb of your RAM when loaded.

The AI space continues to evolve at a wild pace, with developments like this becoming more common.

## Key Takeaways

- But before we open the terminal, we need to talk about why this is worth doing at all.
- Most of the time, cloud models are better and easier.
- I’m not going to pretend an 8-billion parameter model on a laptop beats frontier AI.
