## The Details

So, one would assume that the future of AI depends on building increasingly powerful processors! Many of today’s AI systems are not limited they can perform calculations.

They are limited they can access data. Data that is essential for them to perform their calculations.

## Why This Matters

In other words, the future of AI may depend less on computation and more on memory. I know that may not seem intuitive, but let’s take a step back and imagine something: imagine hiring the world’s most efficient and fastest chef. This chef can prepare meals at incredible speed.
