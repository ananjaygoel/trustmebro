You name it, Ive been in the weeds with it. But somewhere along the way, I kicked off getting curious about what happens before the data lands on my desk.

## Why This Matters

What does the infrastructure behind all of this actually look like? That curiosity planted a seed. Then AI kicked off making a lot of what I do faster and easier.

The AI space continues to evolve at a wild pace, with developments like this becoming more common.
