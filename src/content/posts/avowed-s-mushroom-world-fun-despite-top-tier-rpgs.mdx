## What's Happening Obsidian's anticipated fantasy RPG, Avowed, delivered a 'good time' in 2025, according to our reviewer. While it doesn't claim to match the depth of genre giants, it certainly provided an enjoyable experience. The game's world of Eora, described as 'colourful and varied,' captured attention with its sprawling coastlines, dense forests, arid deserts, and notable abundance of mushrooms. Combat also proved 'satisfying enough,' making each opportunity to engage something to anticipate. ## Why This Matters This perspective highlights that an RPG doesn't always need to redefine the genre to be worthwhile. Avowed's ability to offer a 'good time' suggests a focus on engaging exploration and solid gameplay mechanics. The rich, varied environments of Eora, from its vast deserts to its distinctive mushroom biomes, were key to keeping players engaged. Coupled with satisfying combat, the game successfully whet the appetite for adventure. - Baldur's Gate
