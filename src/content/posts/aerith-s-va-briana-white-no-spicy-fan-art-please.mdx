## What's Happening Briana White, the voice behind Aerith Gainsborough in Final Fantasy 7 Remake, has a clear message for fans: keep your fan art and fiction appropriate. She's explicitly stated she doesn't want to be tagged in anything "spicy" or "raunchy." This request comes as many voice actors and public figures grapple with boundaries in online fan communities. White's stance highlights a growing need for respectful interaction, even when celebrating beloved characters. ## Why This Matters This isn't just about one actor; it's a broader conversation about consent and boundaries in online spaces. Just because someone voices a character doesn't mean they've consented to be associated with all fan interpretations. The issue often blurs lines between the character and the person portraying them. Fans need to remember there's a real individual behind the voice, deserving of personal space and respect. - **Sets a precedent:** Other VAs might feel more empowered to voice similar concerns.
- **Promotes healthier fan culture:** Encourages fans to think critically about the content they create and share.
- **Raises awareness:** Highlights the mental and emotional toll public figures can face from unsolicited content. ## The Bottom Line Ultimately, this is a call for digital etiquette and empathy within fandoms. It's a reminder that even in celebrating creativity, respect for individuals should always come first. Can online communities truly learn to self-regulate and foster healthier environments for everyone involved?
