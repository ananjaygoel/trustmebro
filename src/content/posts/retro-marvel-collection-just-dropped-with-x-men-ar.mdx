Plus to undeniable classic X-Men: The Arcade Game , the Marvel MaXimum Collection will also include titles like Captain America and The Avengers and the infamous Silver Surfer game for NES. Marvel MaXimum Collection includes six old-school Marvel titles across 13 versions of those games, which were originally dropped for the arcade, Game Boy, Game Gear, NES, Sega Genesis, and/or Super NES.

## Why This Matters

While the big draw may be Konami's influential X-Men arcade game dropped in 1992 — which was digitally rereleased on PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 back in 2010 — there's a lot here for Spider-Man the fandom too. Spider-Man/Venom: Maximum Carnage , Venom/Spider-Man: Separation Anxiety , and Spider-Man/X-Men: Arcade’s Revenge are also included as part of the collection.
