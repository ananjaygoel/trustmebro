## New Releases on Netflix This December

Lowkey, Netflix has been serving up the fire content lately, and December is no exception. We're talking about the A-listers, folks - Kate Winslet, George Clooney, Adam Sandler, and more are all starring in Netflix originals this month.

It's giving 'main character energy' just thinking about it.

## Must-See Romantic Comedies  If you're looking for something to watch with your bae, we've got you covered. Netflix has got a solid lineup of rom-coms this month, including 'The Lost City' starring Sandra Bullock and Channing Tatum. We're highkey excited to see the chemistry between these two leads, and we're lowkey hoping for a sequel. The movie follows a reclusive romance novelist who goes on a book tour with her cover model, only to find herself falling for him.

## Netflix Originals with the Big Stars  We know what you're thinking - what about the big stars? Well, let's talk about Kate Winslet's latest project. The actress is starring in 'Avatar: The Way of Water', but we're actually excited to see her in 'The Palace', a psychological thriller that's got everyone talking. And if you're a fan of Adam Sandler, you're in luck. The comedian is starring in 'Hustle', a sports drama that's all about a basketball scout who discovers a talented player from Spain.

## More Netflix Originals to Watch  We're not done yet, folks. Here are a few more Netflix originals to add to your watchlist:

* 'The Bubble' - a comedy starring Karen Gillan and Leslie Mann, set on the COVID-19 pandemic.
* 'Red Notice' - an action-comedy starring Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson, Ryan Reynolds, and Gal Gadot.
* 'The Unforgivable' - a drama starring Sandra Bullock, about a woman who is released from prison after serving time for a crime she committed as a teenager.

## What to Watch This December  It's understood that we can't possibly watch everything on Netflix, but we're giving you a heads up on the best shows to binge this month.
