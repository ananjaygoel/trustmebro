## The Details

Beloved British astronomer Guy Ottewell turned 90 on . EarthSky wishes him a warm congrats on completing another trip around the sun.

Guy Ottewell is best known worldwide for his clean astronomy charts and hand-illustrated yearly Astronomical Calendar, of which 2026 will be the final year. Virtually every astronomy educator knows Guys calendar and employs it in teaching about the night sky and outer space.

## Why This Matters

Millions have benefited from Ottewells unique view of outer space. Ottewells Astronomical Calendar was popular as a printed book from 1974 to 2016. Thousands of sky-lovers in more than 100 countries purchased it.
