## Why This Matters

Visually it clearly pulls from the original Game Boy Pokemon games, right down to the protag's little red cap, but still finds its own visual identity. It's the kind of pixel art that feels like an evolution as opposed to a pastiche, with some lovely colours and, thankfully, monster designs (although the music could maybe do a better job at distinguishing itself).
