## Why This Matters

No shark movie is going to outdo Jaws relatively straightforward story about a shark who eats swimmers at a beach, and there are countless films of varying quality about sharks circling people who are stranded at sea, like Open Water and The Reef . Instead, many modern shark movies turn to unexpected settings or gimmick-driven twists to give their shark flicks some bite. Rather than try to outdo Steven Spielberg, shark moviemakers often rely on gaudy hooks.
