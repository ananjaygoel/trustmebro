## What's Happening Milano’s Odd Job Collection, a rare PS1 title from 1999, has made a surprising comeback. This unique game blends minigame and life simulation elements, originally designed with young girls in mind. It features an 11-year-old protagonist named Milano, living out a story straight from a slice-of-life anime. Despite its age, the game is now widely available after years of obscurity. ## Why This Matters The reappearance of Milano’s Odd Job Collection highlights how hidden gems from console eras can find new audiences.
