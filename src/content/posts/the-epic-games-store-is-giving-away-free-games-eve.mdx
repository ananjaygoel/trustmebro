## What's Happening

Okay so The Epic Games Store continues to give away a free game each week, and this week is another nice find with a price that can't be beaten.

Every Thursday at 8 AM PT / 11 AM ET, Epic offers at least one free PC game (and sometimes even as many as two or three). All you need to do to claim the free games is create a free Epic account and enable two-factor authentication. (shocking, we know)
