## The Details

Just out of curiosity, I kept track of new NAS enclosures, and what surprised me the most was the price of an empty enclosure. That’s because I knew what all would go into making it a complete system with NAS-specific drives and other critical upgrades.

The figure itself wasn’t that big, but I quickly realized that for the same amount of money, I could get a lot more elsewhere. I wrapped up up with a fully working, all-flash NAS, one that is clearly faster and quieter at the same time.
