## Why This Matters

I've played Sektori myself and been similarly entranced. It's close to Geometry Wars on a superficial level; it's an arcade twin-stick shooter where you pilot a little ship around enclosed spaces while blasting away hordes of colourful, encroaching, simple-shaped enemies. And that frantic feeling of overwhelm that made Geometry Wars so thrilling, both to play and watch?
